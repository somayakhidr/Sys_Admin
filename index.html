How We Are Different

Save Paws (SP) is a Egyption registered charity that dives in to help and re-home at-risk animals affected by natural disasters and climate change. We are foster-based, shelter free rescue and and provide our rescue animals loving temporary care while working endlessly to find them their fur-ever homes through a careful screening process.

Save Paws based

Our animals live in a loving home environment while we get to know them and find their forever homes!

No Animal is Hopeless

We have a special affinity for animals with medical and physical needs, with a particular adoration for animals who have visual and hearing impairments.

Climate Change & Natural Disasters

OUR team word

Our mission extends beyond the confines of these walls. We are advocates for responsible pet ownership, tirelessly working to educate our community on the importance of spaying, neutering, vaccinations, and the lifelong commitment required to care for a beloved pet. We believe that through education, we can prevent the suffering of countless animals and promote a more compassionate society..

Mohamed Elhanan Manager
